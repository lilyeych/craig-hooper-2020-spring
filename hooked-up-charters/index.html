Hooked-Up Charters

A website that captures the essence of a day out on a salmon fishing boat, cruising the coastal waters of British Columbia, Canada.

Website
Role: UX + UI Design
2016

The project brief

With so much competition in the Vancouver Island fishing charter industry, and with potential customers researching fishing excursions on mobile devices (and often making last-minute decisions), this client knew they had to stand out from competitors. After a thorough analysis of their site analytics and customer interviews, it was determined that a mobile experience needed to be just as robust as a desktop version, as more traffic was coming from mobile devices.

The result

An experience where the mobile version is just as robust, easy to use and inviting as the desktopâ€”information is clear and easy to find, with none of the visual appeal of the desktop version sacrificed for a smaller screen.
