McKay Guitars

A website for a master luthier who handcrafts guitars that sound as beautiful as they look.

Website
Role: UX & UI design
Produced at: Banfield Agency
2015

The project

The goal was to design an experience that was as elegant as the guitars themselves, on a single page. We used subtle animations and transition effects to give the user the sense that they were experiencing the McKay Guitar story one layer at a time.
